How it works

Three seconds of setup. Then it just watches.

FrostByte sits in your menu bar and only acts when you actually walk away — the moment you come back, your game is full-speed again.

STEP 01

Pick your game

Roblox is on by default. Tick any other app you want cooled while you're away.

STEP 02

Step away

When the game isn't the app you're using, FrostByte waits your chosen time, then throttles it — CPU, heat, and fan noise drop hard.

STEP 03

Stay online

"Cool" mode keeps the game alive so the server never disconnects you. Tested 8+ hours AFK in Roblox.

The fine print, honestly

Yours, private, and no funny business.

🔒 Stays on your Mac

No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. The only network feature is the optional phone remote — served only on your own Wi-Fi.

🛡️ Never touches your files

It cools apps by briefly pausing them with standard macOS signals, and un-pauses the instant you're back. Nothing is deleted or changed.

🧊 Honestly estimated

The savings numbers are clearly labeled estimates — a fun way to see your impact, not a billing meter.
